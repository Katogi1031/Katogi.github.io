What is Parnas Inc.

Parnas is a venture company specializing in rigging services. We provide specialized services for CG production with a view to maximizing the use of the power of AI in the way various services are provided in the world. Global demand for CG production continues to increase for animation, games, and metaverse. In addition, CG production in Japan is concentrated in Tokyo, making it difficult for talented young creators to find work in the regions. "To contribute to Japan's CG content industry by promoting full remote CG production by concentrating on rigging as a specialized service". We have already gathered creators from all over Japan who share this passion and have begun preparations for full-scale operations. Why don't you come to Parnas and put your creative passion and experience to work?

For those who want to work at Parnas

Parnas is actively and continuously looking for riggers / TAs.
If you are looking for a professional services company where you can hone your career, use your expertise to make a difference in the computer graphics industry, and continue to grow as young people grow, please feel free to apply.
