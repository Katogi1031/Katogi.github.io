Privacy Policy

Parnas Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") establishes this Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "the Policy") regarding the handling of personal information of users in the services provided on this website (hereinafter referred to as "the Services").

Article 1 (Personal Information)

"Personal Information" refers to information about a living individual that can identify a specific individual by name, date of birth, address, telephone number, contact information, or other descriptions, as defined by the Personal Information Protection Act in Japan, as well as data on appearance, fingerprints, voiceprints, and information that can identify a specific individual from the information itself, such as the insurance policy number of a health insurance card (personal identification information).

Article 2 (Collection Method of Personal Information)

The Company may ask users for personal information such as name, date of birth, address, telephone number, email address, bank account number, credit card number, driver's license number, etc. when they register to use the Services. In addition, the Company may collect transaction records and payment information including personal information of users from its partners (information providers, advertisers, ad distribution partners, etc.).

Article 3 (Purpose of Collecting and Using Personal Information)

The purposes for which the Company collects and uses personal information are as follows:

  1. To provide and operate the Company's services.
  2. To respond to inquiries from users (including verifying the identity of the user).
  3. To send emails regarding new features, updates, campaigns, and other services provided by the Company that users are currently using.
  4. To contact users as necessary for maintenance and important notifications.
  5. To identify users who violate the terms of use or who attempt to use the service for fraudulent or improper purposes and to deny their use of the service.
  6. To allow users to view and modify their registration information, delete their information, and view their usage history.
  7. To charge users for the use of paid services.
  8. Other purposes incidental to the above purposes.

Article 4 (Changes to the Purpose of Use)

The Company may change the purpose of use of personal information only when the new purpose is reasonably related to the original purpose. If the purpose of use is changed, the Company will notify users of the new purpose by a method prescribed by the Company, or by posting it on this website.

Article 5 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

Except in cases permitted by the Personal Information Protection Act and other laws and regulations, the Company will not provide personal information to third parties without the prior consent of the user, except in the following cases:

  1. When it is difficult to obtain the consent of the user and it is necessary to provide personal information to improve public health or promote the healthy development of children.
  2. When it is necessary to cooperate with a national agency, local government, or person entrusted by them to carry out the affairs stipulated by law, and obtaining the consent of the user may impede the execution of such affairs.
  3. When the following matters are notified or announced in advance, and the Company has reported to the Personal Information Protection Commission:
    1. Inclusion of provision to third parties in the purpose of use
    2. Items of data to be provided to third parties
    3. Method or means of providing to third parties
    4. Procedure for responding to requests from the individual to stop providing personal information to third parties
    5. Method of accepting requests from the individual
  4. Notwithstanding the provisions of the preceding paragraph, the following cases shall not be considered as provision of personal information to third parties:
    1. When the handling of personal information in whole or in part is entrusted to a third party within the scope necessary for achieving the purpose of use by the Company.
    2. When personal information is jointly used with a specific person, and the user has been notified or made easily aware in advance of the purpose of use, items of personal information to be jointly used, the scope of persons who will jointly use the information, the purpose of use of those who will jointly use the information, and the name or title of the person responsible for managing the personal information jointly used.

Article 6 (Disclosure of Personal Information)

  1. When we receive a request from an individual to disclose their personal information, we will promptly disclose such information to the individual. However, if disclosure falls under any of the following, we may choose not to disclose all or part of the information and will promptly notify the individual of such a decision. Please note that a fee of JPY 1,000 per case will be charged for disclosure of personal information.
    1. If there is a risk of harming the life, body, property, or other rights and interests of the individual or a third party
    2. If there is a risk of significantly impeding the proper implementation of our business
    3. If it is otherwise in violation of the law
  2. Regardless of the preceding provision, we will not disclose information other than personal information such as history and characteristic information as a general rule.

Article 7 (Correction and Deletion of Personal Information)

  1. If an individual's personal information held by us is incorrect, the individual may request correction, addition, or deletion (hereinafter referred to as "correction, etc.") by following our prescribed procedures.
  2. If we determine that it is necessary to respond to such a request from the individual in accordance with the preceding paragraph, we will promptly carry out the correction, etc. of the relevant personal information.
  3. If we have carried out correction, etc. in accordance with the preceding paragraph or have made a decision not to carry out correction, etc., we will promptly notify the individual of such decision.

Article 8 (Suspension of Use, etc. of Personal Information)

  1. If an individual requests us to suspend or delete the use of personal information due to the reason that the personal information is being handled beyond the scope of the purpose of use or has been acquired by fraudulent means, we will promptly conduct necessary investigations.
  2. If we determine that it is necessary to respond to such a request in accordance with the investigation results of the preceding paragraph, we will promptly suspend or delete the use of the relevant personal information.
  3. If we have carried out the suspension of use, etc. in accordance with the preceding paragraph or have made a decision not to carry out such suspension, we will promptly notify the individual of such decision.
  4. Notwithstanding the preceding two paragraphs, if it is difficult to suspend the use, etc. due to a large cost or other reasons, or if it is necessary to take alternative measures to protect the individual's rights and interests, we will take such alternative measures.

Article 9 (Changes to Privacy Policy)

  1. We may change the contents of this policy without notifying the user, except for matters stipulated separately in this policy or by law.
  2. Unless otherwise specified by us, the revised privacy policy will take effect from the time it is posted on this website.
