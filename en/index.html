Feature About services of Parnas

Ⅰ. A specialized group of Rigging

A lot of CG production companies offer comprehensive services, undertaking everything from modeling to rigging to animation. Among them, we employ only riggers and actively share and accumulate know-how as a team to provide speed and quality that only a specialized service can.

Ⅱ. Specializing in movie production

Among our CG productions, we focus on cell-look video production using Maya. We have a wide range of applications in video production and can assist out clients in the production of in-game video, amusement, metaverse, and advertising.

Ⅲ. Fully remote work

We are commited to developing a full remote working environment, and we are looking for the best and brightest talent from all over Japan. We provide long-term sustainable services by eliminating CG resource issues concentrated in Tokyo and at the same time helping to create jobs in rural areas.
